  \begin{enumerate}
  \def\labelenumii{\arabic{enumii}.}
  \tightlist
  \item

  \end{enumerate}

  \begin{enumerate}
  \def\labelenumii{\arabic{enumii}.}
  \setcounter{enumii}{1}
  \tightlist
  \item

  \end{enumerate}

\section{Bookdown Simple Explanation}\label{bookdown-simple-explanation}

Here is a general summary of how Bookdown creates html websites from .Rmd files.
